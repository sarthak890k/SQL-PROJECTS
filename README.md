# Customer and Product Analysis Using SQL


##  Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior and product performance using SQL. The objective is to extract meaningful business insights that can support data-driven decision-making in areas such as customer analysis , product optimization, and revenue growth.

The analysis demonstrates practical SQL skills commonly used in Business Analyst and Data Analyst roles.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- MySQL
- SQL
- MySQL Workbench

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes information related to:

- Customer ID
- Customer Name
- Product ID
- Product Name
- Product Category
- Order Date
- Quantity Purchased
- Unit Price
- Sales Amount

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Evaluate product category performance.
- Understand customer spending patterns.
- Generate actionable business insights for decision-making.

---

- Aggregate Functions

- Subqueries

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- A small percentage of customers generated a significant portion of total revenue.
- Certain product categories consistently outperformed others in sales.
- Top-selling products contributed heavily to overall business performance.
- Customer purchasing behavior revealed opportunities for targeted marketing.
- Product performance analysis identified both high-performing and underperforming products.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing campaigns on high-value customers.
- Promote top-performing products through cross-selling and upselling.
- Review underperforming products for pricing, demand, or inventory improvements.
- Improve customer retention through loyalty programs and personalized offers.
- Monitor product category performance regularly to support inventory planning.
